Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Who We Are

nogoon ("we", "us", "our") operates the website nogoon.io and provides a porn-blocking script for macOS and Windows.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect as little data as possible:

  • Payment data: When you purchase the permanent version, your payment is processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number. Stripe provides us with a transaction ID and payment status.
  • Analytics: We use Vercel Analytics, which collects anonymous, aggregated page view data. No cookies are used. No personal information is collected.
  • Execution tracking: When you run the free trial or paid script, a lightweight beacon records the event (operating system, timestamp). No personal identifiers are sent.

3. What Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or accounts
  • No browsing history or browsing behavior
  • No IP addresses stored
  • No cookies placed on your device
  • No data sold to third parties — ever

4. What the Script Does on Your Device

The script modifies local system settings on your computer:

  • Sets DNS servers to CleanBrowsing's adult content filter
  • Adds adult domain entries to your system's hosts file
  • Enables SafeSearch on Google and Bing

No software is installed. No background process runs. No data is sent from your computer after execution (except the one-time tracking beacon).

5. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — hosting and analytics (Vercel Privacy Policy)
  • CleanBrowsing — DNS filtering (CleanBrowsing Privacy Policy)

6. Data Retention

Stripe transaction records are retained as required by financial regulations. Anonymous analytics data is retained by Vercel per their standard policy. We do not maintain any personal database.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is typically nothing to request deletion of. If you have concerns, contact us at support@nogoon.io.

8. Children

This product is intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

Questions? Email support@nogoon.io.
