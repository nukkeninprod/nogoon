## Video Production Tips

1. **Screen recordings** — QuickTime screen record, crop to vertical (9:16)
2. **Face cam optional** — trust goes up with face, but screen-only works for demos
3. **Use trending sounds** — even under screen recordings, the sound boosts reach
4. **Text on screen** — assume sound off. Every key point should be readable
5. **Green screen effect** — your face over the terminal. TikTok loves this format
6. **Batch record** — film 10-20 videos in one sitting. Schedule with Later or TikTok's built-in scheduler
